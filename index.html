Invisalign

Everyone deserves a confident smile, and Invisalign clear aligners can help you achieve just that! Invisalign is a popular orthodontic treatment that uses a series of clear, customized aligners to gradually straighten teeth. Because the aligners are nearly invisible, many patients choose Invisalign over traditional braces. In addition, the aligners are removable, so you can eat whatever you want and brush and floss your teeth with ease. Best of all, the treatment is typically faster and more comfortable than braces.

Dental Veneers

When it comes to achieving a beautiful, healthy smile, veneers are an excellent long-term solution. Dr. Dr.Marcus Denny utilizes digital design software and a master dental ceramist to create customize, natural looking veneers that fit harmoniously into the face.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are the best way to replace missing teeth and it’s the same biological process as orthopedic surgeons use to reconstruct hips, knees, and shoulders. The titanium or zirconia implant is placed into the jawbone to support one or more false teeth, called crowns. Implants are an attractive and durable option for replacing missing teeth, and they can also help to preserve the health of the surrounding teeth.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Full mouth rehabilitation is a comprehensive approach to repairing and restoring the function and appearance of the teeth. This can be done for a variety of reasons, including tooth loss, decay, injury, or wear and tear. Full mouth rehabilitation typically includes a combination of treatments, such as dental implants, crowns, veneers, and bridges. The goal of full mouth rehabilitation is to improve the health and function of the teeth while also creating a natural-looking smile. With our full mouth rehabilitation services, we can restore your smile and improve your overall quality of life.

THE NEW YOU

VENEERS

Dr.Marcus Denny practices Facial Aesthetic Dentistry which means he designs the teeth to fit the face. He identifies facial landmarks and facial asymmetries to design teeth that complement and create beauty within the face. Dr.Marcus Denny Dental Aesthetics works with an in house master ceramist so he can design customized smiles using the Facial Aesthetic Dentistry approach.

The veneers process always begins with the initial consultation:

First Appointment: Dr. Dr.Marcus Denny fits a temporary smile to the patient. He then minimally shapes and fits their teeth for the temporary veneers they will wear for 1-2 weeks. This allows the patient to test drive their new, improved smile.

Second Appointment: After living with the temporary smile, the patient returns to provide feedback on the look and fit of their veneers. Using this feedback, a master ceramist makes a new mold before creating the final veneers.

Third Appointment: Dr.Marcus Denny fits the patient with their permanent veneers. At this time, he removes the temporary veneers and the patient receives their new, permanent smile.
